About Us

The Kuching Waterfront Lodge is located at Main Bazaar, one of the oldest & busiest streets in Kuching where trading took place since a long time ago in the era of James Brooke, The White Rajah. Today, the Main Bazaar is still buzzing with people, entertainment & business activities, traditional spice shops, antiques,

The lodge sits right in the heart of Kuching, where the old meets the new. The splendid waterfront is right in front of the doorstep. A stone's throw away is the Tua Pek Kong Temple, shopping malls & pubs.

Here at the lodge, we hope to provide you with a safe, enjoyable & comfortable stay so that you can experience fully what the historic city of Kuching can offer you.
